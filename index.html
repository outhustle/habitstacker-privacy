Privacy Policy

Habit Stacker · Last updated: April 2026

Habit Stacker is a fully offline app. Your data never leaves your device.

Data Collection

Habit Stacker does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All habit data, completion records, and settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage frameworks. We have no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.

Advertisements

The free version displays ads served by Google AdMob. Google may collect device identifiers and usage data per their own privacy policy. Premium users see no ads.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service is Google AdMob for advertising in the free tier. No other SDKs, analytics, or tracking tools are included.

Children's Privacy

Habit Stacker does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes

Updates to this policy will be posted here with a new date.
